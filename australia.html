Strict Eligibility Criteria

  • Work Experience

    Minimum 5 years of full-time relevant work experience.

  • Program Level

    Applicants must apply to Master's programs only.

  • English Proficiency

    Minimum IELTS score of 6.5.

  • Return Condition

    After completing the degree, scholars MUST return to Bangladesh and stay for at least 2 years.

Document Formatting Rules

The official document templates and formats are provided in Word files directly on the scholarship website. Ensure you use ONLY their provided templates for CVs, LORs, etc.
