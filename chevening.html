Eligibility & Conditions

  • Undergraduate Degree: Equivalent to a UK 2:1 classification.
  • Course Duration: Usually 1-year Master's program.
  • Work Experience: Minimum 2 years.

    Can include full-time, part-time, internship, volunteer, paid, or unpaid. Does not have to be related to your previous field (but must explain career change).

  • Post-Scholarship Condition:

    Must return to Bangladesh and work there for at least 2 years. Graduate Route Visa is NOT allowed.

Required Documents

Note on IELTS: Not mandatory for the scholarship itself, but usually required by the university.

Reference Letters (2 Required)

Must be uploaded after receiving an interview call. Checked by the British Embassy.

Address to: The British High Commissioner, Dhaka

Must Include:

  • Referee contact details (address, email, phone)
  • How long they've known you & relationship capacity
  • Date of last contact
  • Endorsement covering: Leadership, Networking, Skills, Personal/Intellectual qualities
