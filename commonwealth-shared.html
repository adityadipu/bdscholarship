Commonwealth Shared Scholarship (UK)

Track your dual application process: University Admissions & the CSC Portal.

Critical Rule: Dual Application Required Fully Funded

Unlike the main Commonwealth Scholarship, you do not apply via the UGC. Instead, you must apply directly to participating UK universities AND submit a separate application on the Commonwealth Scholarship portal. There is no strict limit on the number of applications you can make.

Official CSC Shared Website

The 6 Development Themes

You MUST choose a subject aligned with one of these themes. Your selection will be evaluated based on Academic Merit, Development Impact, and Leadership Potential.

Key Conditions

  • No Job Experience Required: Fresh graduates can apply.
  • No Initial Offer Letter Required: You don't need the university offer at the time of initial application, but you will need it eventually.
  • Return Condition: Must return to Bangladesh after completing studies.
  • No Part-Time Jobs: Working during the scholarship period is strictly not allowed.
