Commonwealth Scholarship (UK)

Track your UGC Bangladesh nomination, 6 development themes, and document submission.

Crucial: Bangladesh UGC Route Fully Funded

You cannot apply directly to the UK initially. You must first apply through the University Grants Commission (UGC), Bangladesh for nomination. No offer letter and no job experience are required at the time of application!

Official CSC Website Guidance Document

The 6 Development Themes

Applicants MUST choose a subject directly aligned with one of the following themes. Your application essays must justify this alignment.

Academic Requirements

CGPA Classification:

First Class: CGPA 4.00

Upper Second Class: CGPA 3.30 - 3.90

Lower Second Class: CGPA 2.70 - 3.30

Academic results must clearly show:
Total marks, Obtained marks, and Final classification (Excellent / Very Good / Good / Pass).

Key Scholarship Conditions:

  • Must return to Bangladesh after completing studies.
  • Part-time jobs are NOT allowed during the scholarship period.

Document Checklist

Academic certificates and transcripts must be notarised copies.
