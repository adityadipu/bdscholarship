Eligibility & Timeline

  • Timeline: Opens in December, Deadline in January (varies by program).
  • Age Limit: Below 35 years for Master's programs.
  • Language: IELTS/TOEFL/HSK not always mandatory. Medium is English (some require 1-year Chinese course).

Scholarship Categories

Type A - Chinese Embassy Track

  • Agency Number for Bangladesh: 0501
  • Announced via official BD circular.
  • Can select up to 3 universities.

Type B - University Track

  • Apply directly to a Chinese university.
  • Agency number depends on the university.
  • Often requires contacting professors beforehand.
