Critical Submission Rules & Links

You must apply directly to the university, not to DAAD. Some programs require physical courier submission. Always send notarised copies, NEVER original documents.

Basic Eligibility

  • Education: Must hold a Bachelor's Degree.
  • Work Experience: Minimum 2 years of relevant work experience.
  • Study Gap: Not more than 6 years since graduation.
  • Language: IELTS score (depends on specific program requirements).
