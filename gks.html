Global Korea Scholarship (GKS)

Track your 3-year Master's program application, mandatory hardcopy submissions, and required forms.

Critical Rule: Hardcopy Submission ONLY Fully Funded

There is no online application for GKS. All applications must be sent by physical post (hard copy). The scholarship duration is 3 years (1 year Korean language training + 2 years Master's study).

Study in Korea Official Website

Application Tracks (Choose ONE)

1. Embassy Track

  • Apply via: Korean Embassy in Bangladesh
  • Deadline: Around February
  • Quota: ~5 candidates
  • Max 3 university choices (At least one must be Type B).

2. University Track

  • Apply via: Directly to Korean universities
  • Deadline: Feb - Apr (varies by university)
  • Quota: ~9 candidates
  • If you fail Embassy Track Round 1, you CAN apply to Univ Track in the same year.

Eligibility & Extra Points

Minimum CGPA: 2.64

IELTS / TOEFL: Not mandatory

Korean Language: Not required initially

You receive EXTRA PREFERENCE if you have:

TOPIK Level 3 or higher
IELTS/TOEFL Score
Science/Engineering Bg
Low-income family bg

Strict Essay Formatting Rules

Font: Times New Roman Size: 11 Spacing: Single

Personal Statement (Form 2) - 1 Page

  • Motivation for applying & Family/Education bg.
  • Significant experiences, achievements.
  • Influential people/events in your life.
  • Extracurriculars (clubs, volunteering, work).

Statement of Purpose - Max 2 Pages

  • 1. Language Plan: How you'll learn Korean in Yr 1.
  • 2. Study Plan: Why this field? Plan for Yr 1, Yr 2, Final Yr.
  • 3. Future Plan: Career goals, sector, how GKS helps.

Recommendation Letter (Form 5)

Requires Two recommendation letters (Prof, Dept Head, Employer, or Supervisor).

Crucial Submission Format:
For EACH letter, you must submit:
1 Original + 3 Photocopies (Total 4 copies per letter).

The letters MUST include a signature, institutional seal, and be inside a sealed envelope.
