MEXT Scholarship (Japan)

Track your Japanese Government Scholarship application, required documents, and submission steps.

Fully Funded Japanese Government Scholarship No App Fee

Covers full tuition fee, monthly stipend, and round-trip airfare. The official circular is typically published in May by the Japanese Embassy in Bangladesh.

Eligibility & Pathways

Must have completed a Bachelor's degree.

Strong academic background (CGPA ~3.5+ preferred).

English/Japanese knowledge beneficial (IELTS/TOEFL NOT mandatory).

Recommendation Types (Choose One)

1. Embassy Recommendation

  • Apply through the Japanese Embassy in BD.
  • Embassy manages screening and placement.
  • Note: Usually no need to contact professors directly.

2. University Recommendation

  • Apply directly to a Japanese university.
  • Requires: Contacting professors & submitting a research proposal directly to them.

Preparation Tips

Practice previous years' written exam questions (English Test is crucial).

Prepare a strong, detailed study plan or research proposal.

Apply in a field closely related to your previous academic background.

Review subject-related materials thoroughly before the oral interview.
