Program Requirements

  • Target Programs: Must choose Master's programs worth exactly 120 credits (2 years).
  • Quantity: Maximum of 4 programs can be selected (same or different universities).
  • English Proficiency: Minimum IELTS score of 6.5.
  • Motivation Letters: Each chosen program requires a separate tailored motivation letter.

Document Checklist

Postal Submission Warning: While most documents are uploaded online, some specific universities may require hard copies sent by post. In this case, send transcripts in one sealed envelope and certificates in another.
